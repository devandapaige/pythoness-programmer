## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Copy the prompt** that matches your need
2. **Fill in the bracketed sections** with your actual content
3. **Paste into your AI tool** (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
4. **Review and refine** the AI suggestions
5. **Test and iterate** based on results

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific:** Include context about your audience, goals, and constraints
- **Provide examples:** Show the AI what you like/don't like from your current site
- **Iterate:** Use follow-up prompts to refine suggestions
- **Maintain brand voice:** Always remind the AI of your brand guidelines
- **Test variations:** Generate multiple options and compare
- **Consider accessibility:** Always include accessibility in your prompts
