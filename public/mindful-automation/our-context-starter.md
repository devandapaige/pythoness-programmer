# O.U.R. Context Starter

*Mindful Automation — May 2026 · Pythoness Programmer*

O.U.R. Context is not the same as the Y.O.U.R. Framework. It is the file pattern for giving AI assistants, collaborators, and your future self a home on disk:

- **O — Organized:** Patterns live somewhere you can point to
- **U — Usable:** Portable, plain-text, readable without logging into five dashboards
- **R — Recurring:** Context you maintain once and reuse

Copy this template into your IDE workbench folder. Name it `context.md`, `AGENTS.md`, or whatever fits your setup.
