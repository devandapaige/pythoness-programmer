# Shedding Big Tech in the Wooden Snake year: a year of wisdom, a prairie burn ahead

## "The dead skin you shed becomes the kindling for what comes next."

Hey there, cosmic coders and digital boundary-setters.

The Wooden Snake year is ending. And I need to talk about what it actually meant.

Not in theory. Not in horoscope platitudes. I mean what it meant in practice, in real choices, in accounts deleted and platforms abandoned and a growing sense of peace that comes from watching the tech you used to depend on reveal exactly what it always was.

This post is my final reflection on the Wooden Snake year, a summary of everything I've written, learned, and shed over the past twelve months. It's also a goodbye to Reddit. And it's a first look at what the Fire Horse year is about to burn down.

## The three principles that guided this year

When I wrote my [Lunar New Year post](/blog/lunar-new-year-2025) in January 2025, I outlined the Wooden Snake's core wisdom:

1. **Observe before you judge** - Patient observation before action
2. **Adapt to the tool's true nature** - Work with reality, not against it
3. **Find smarter paths forward** - Creative solutions that serve your actual goals

At the time, I was thinking about DeepSeek rivaling ChatGPT at a fraction of the cost, about open-source communities outpacing corporate AI labs, about how scarcity pushes better solutions. I wrote: "True power isn't about brute force, it's about adapting, observing, and finding smarter paths forward."

I didn't fully understand yet that those principles were also about to guide me through a complete divestment from Big Tech platforms. That the shedding would be personal, political, and irreversible.

## The full timeline of the shed

Here's what the Wooden Snake year actually looked like, month by month:

**January-February 2025: The first tongue-flick.** I wrote about [Elon Musk's Social Security misinformation](/blog/on-elon-musk-and-dates) and called out the dangerous intersection of tech illiteracy and political power. I published [Sawdust and Sacred Stones](/blog/sawdust-and-sacred-stones), a historical fiction piece about corporate exploitation and indigenous resilience that reflected the same power dynamics playing out in Silicon Valley. The Wooden Snake was already tasting the air, sensing what needed to go.

**March 2025: Building sustainable alternatives.** In my [Digital Sustainability](/blog/digital-sustainability-that-wont-burn-you-out) newsletter, I started building the actual replacement systems. The 40/20/20 Pomodoro method for neurodivergent brains. Finch for sustainable self-care tracking. Sunsama for task management. No notifications on my phone. No e-commerce apps. I was constructing the scaffolding I'd need once the old platforms fell away.

**April 2025: The spring cleaning.** The [Digital Spring Cleaning](/blog/digital-spring-cleaning-a-month-of-grit-and-growth) series wasn't just about tidying up digital clutter. It was the GRIT Framework applied to a deeper question: which tools bring joy and which ones extract from you? The answer was clarifying. I made the switch to Obsidian, built my first Firefox extension, and started seeing my digital life through the lens of what actually serves my brain versus what serves a platform's engagement metrics.

**May 2025: Mindful automation as resistance.** The [Mindful Automation](/blog/mindful-automation-systems) series was about building workflows that respect my energy patterns. But underneath that, it was about building independence. Every automation I created that didn't rely on a Big Tech platform was another thread cut. Make.com instead of Google's ecosystem. Cal.com instead of Google Calendar. Obsidian instead of Google Docs. Slow, patient, deliberate. Wooden Snake energy.

**July 2025: Setting boundaries.** The [Tech Boundaries](/blog/tech-boundaries-brain-friendly-systems) resource codified what I'd been learning all year: your digital systems should work with your brain, not extract from it. The three pillars (design your space, automate for your energy, build sustainable habits) were the Wooden Snake's principles translated into practical action.

**August-September 2025: Seeing clearly.** The [AI Myth-Busting](/blog/ai-mythbusting-critical-thinking-toolkit) toolkit applied the Wooden Snake's wisdom directly to AI: observe before you trust, adapt to new information, find smarter paths forward. Then [Back to Basics](/blog/back-to-basics-ai-ready-workflows) took the Snake's patience principle and applied it to digital organization. The four steps I kept returning to: Observe, Adapt, Transform, Prepare.

**October 2025: Privacy as pleasure.** The [Privacy Pleasure](/blog/privacy-pleasure-digital-independence) resource framed digital privacy as self-care in a world where billionaires monetize every click. Password security, VPN protection, encrypted communication, complete system integration. Four weeks of building a digital life that actually belongs to you.

**January 2026: The final shed.** I wrote two posts that crystallized the entire year. In [Off TikTok For Good](/blog/off-tiktok-for-good-tech-ethics-and-boundaries), I announced my complete boycott of tech produced or owned by Larry Ellison, Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, and financed by Jared Kushner. TikTok gone. All Meta platforms gone. And in [Rest Mode](/blog/rest-mode-creativity-and-the-wooden-snake-shed), I described how six months of daily bullet journaling had become the physical record of the Wooden Snake's transformation. Scanning documents, shredding paper, creating space. The final shed.

## But the shed isn't done yet: goodbye, Reddit

I'm deleting my Reddit accounts.

All of them. Including r/pythoness_reposts, which I was still listing as a place to find me as recently as last month.

Here's why: in February 2025, The Intercept reported that the Department of Homeland Security had been [using social media posts from Reddit, Facebook, and Google to identify and target individuals who posted anti-ICE content](https://theintercept.com/2025/02/07/ice-raids-social-media-surveillance-reddit-facebook/). This isn't speculation. DHS confirmed it was using social media monitoring tools that scrape platforms for content related to immigration enforcement, then feeding that data into targeting decisions.

Reddit's 2024 IPO filing revealed the company had [signed a $60 million annual deal with Google to license user content for AI training](https://www.reuters.com/technology/reddit-ai-content-licensing-deal-google-worth-60-million-annually-sources-say-2024-02-22/). Your posts, your comments, your communities, all packaged and sold. And now that same data pipeline that feeds Google's AI models is accessible to government surveillance operations.

I got past the itchy phase. That's the thing about shedding, the part nobody talks about. When a snake sheds its skin, there's a period where the old skin is loose and irritating but not quite ready to come off. You miss the platform. You wonder if you made the right call. You reach for the app out of habit. But once the shed is complete, there's this clarity. This sense of breathing room. The new skin underneath is clean and sensitive and entirely yours.

Reddit was my last holdout. The place where I told myself the community was different, the content was valuable, the tradeoffs were acceptable. But watching a platform sell user data to AI companies while that same data pipeline feeds government surveillance operations? That's not a tradeoff. That's complicity.

## The Wooden Snake's real lesson: follow the money to the source

The Wooden Snake's tongue flicks in two directions. It tastes both what's in front of it and what's behind. Looking back at the full year of shedding, a pattern emerges that goes deeper than individual platform decisions.

Peter Thiel co-founded PayPal in 1998. From that company came the network that now controls much of Silicon Valley: Elon Musk (Tesla, SpaceX, X), Reid Hoffman (LinkedIn), Max Levchin (Affirm), and Thiel himself (Palantir, Facebook's first outside investor). Thiel's Palantir [holds contracts worth billions with ICE, the CIA, and the Department of Defense](https://www.cnbc.com/2024/08/05/palantir-is-the-ai-stock-that-big-tech-ceos-are-envious-of.html) for data analysis and surveillance tools. He was Facebook's first outside investor and sat on its board until 2022.

This isn't conspiracy. It's corporate structure. The same PayPal network that built the platforms we're shedding also built the surveillance infrastructure those platforms now feed into. When I wrote in my [TikTok boycott post](/blog/off-tiktok-for-good-tech-ethics-and-boundaries) that more and more of the same people are holding all the cards, this is what I meant. TikTok's U.S. operations sold to Larry Ellison's Oracle and Jared Kushner's investment group. Kushner's Affinity Partners [backed by Saudi billions](https://fortune.com/2025/12/08/jared-kushner-paramount-warner-netflix-trump-ellison-saudi-antitrust/) bidding for Warner Bros. Discovery and Electronic Arts. Meta [training AI on user data](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/23/meta-internal-research-social-media-harm-court-filing.html) while burying research about harm to teens. Reddit selling your posts to Google while DHS scrapes the results.

The Wooden Snake observes. And what I observed this year is that these aren't separate problems. They're the same network, the same money, the same consolidation of power. Shedding one platform while staying on another owned by the same people is like shedding only half your skin.

## Now the Fire Horse enters: what burns next

The Chinese zodiac year of the Fire Horse begins now, February 2026. And here's what the Wooden Snake's year of patient shedding has prepared us for: the dead skin we left behind is about to catch fire.

This isn't metaphor for metaphor's sake. Think about what happens during a prairie burn. The accumulated dead matter, the dry grass, the shed skin, the things that served their purpose and were released, all of it becomes fuel. The fire looks destructive. It looks sudden. But the conditions were building for years. And when the burn is complete, the soil is richer than before. The nitrogen floods back into the earth. New growth pushes through in ways that weren't possible while the dead matter was smothering everything.

Big Tech's prairie burn is coming. The structural issues have been accumulating for 10-15 years:

**The litigation is escalating.** [30+ U.S. states are suing Meta](https://www.reuters.com/legal/meta-must-face-us-state-lawsuits-over-teen-social-media-addiction-2024-10-15/) over designing addictive features targeting teens. Internal Meta documents show researchers acknowledged "IG is a drug... we're basically pushers." The [FTC v. Meta case](https://promarket.org/2026/01/22/how-ftc-v-meta-reshapes-the-debate-on-social-media-and-first-amendment-protections) is reshaping the debate about platform accountability and First Amendment protections. These cases have been building for years. The Fire Horse year is when they ignite.

**The EU is targeting addictive design itself.** The European Union has been [exploring legal action against features like infinite scrolling](https://www.euronews.com/next/2025/07/15/eu-considers-ban-on-infinite-scroll-and-autoplay-features), arguing that platforms deliberately engineer compulsive usage patterns. If infinite scrolling becomes legally classified as a manipulative design practice, the business model of every major social platform faces existential disruption.

**Oracle's debt is a tinderbox.** Even as Larry Ellison became the world's second-richest person, Oracle is carrying [massive leverage with negative free cash flow projections](https://fortune.com/2025/12/10/oracle-earnings-may-not-be-enough-to-assuage-debt-ai-deal-fears/) from data center buildouts. The company that now holds TikTok's U.S. operations is financially stretched thin. Fire Horse energy isn't kind to overextended structures.

**The consolidation is unsustainable.** Kushner's Affinity Partners bidding for Warner Bros. Discovery and Electronic Arts with Saudi sovereign wealth money. Ellison's simultaneous positions in Oracle, TikTok, and Paramount/CBS News. The same small group acquiring media, entertainment, social platforms, and surveillance infrastructure. The concentration creates fragility. When one piece burns, the interconnections spread the fire.

**X's terms of service keep shifting.** [Significant updates to X's Terms of Service took effect January 2026](https://privacy.x.com/en/blog/2025/updates-tos-privacy-policy), including changes to content enforcement, user responsibilities, and dispute resolution. Each revision pushes users closer to the exits. Each exit removes revenue. The fire feeds itself.

## The Fire Horse's three principles

The Fire Horse brings a different energy than the Wooden Snake. Where the Snake observed patiently and shed quietly, the Horse charges forward with dynamic force:

1. **Embrace dynamic energy** - The changes coming won't be quiet. They'll be loud, disruptive, and visible. The Fire Horse doesn't shed in private. It burns in public.
2. **Lead with independence** - The divestment work of the Wooden Snake year wasn't just about leaving platforms. It was about building the independent infrastructure to survive what comes next. If you've done the shedding work, you're ready.
3. **Innovate with passion** - The prairie burn doesn't just destroy. It creates the conditions for new growth. Decentralized platforms, open protocols, community-owned infrastructure, privacy-first tools. The innovation that follows the burn will be driven by people who saw the fire coming and built accordingly.

## How the Wooden Snake's wisdom carries forward

Here's what I didn't expect when I started writing about the Wooden Snake in January 2025: the wisdom doesn't expire when the year ends. It transforms.

The patience I learned from observing before judging? In the Fire Horse year, that becomes discernment. The ability to watch the fire and know which parts are necessary destruction and which parts are collateral damage.

The adaptability I practiced by working with tools' true natures? That becomes resilience. When platforms burn, the people who already learned to adapt their workflows will transition fastest.

The smarter paths forward I found by building independent systems? Those become the firebreaks. The infrastructure that survives the burn because it was never dependent on the structures that are burning.

The Wooden Snake's skin doesn't disappear when it's shed. It becomes part of the landscape. And when the Fire Horse's spark hits, that shed skin is the first thing that catches.

## What I'm carrying into the fire

This year, I'll be writing about the Fire Horse's energy with the same honesty I brought to the Wooden Snake year. That means:

- **Documenting the burn.** As Big Tech's structural problems combust into public crises, I'll be tracking what's happening and what it means for people who use these platforms.
- **Building alternatives.** The next phase of Google removal is underway. By end of 2026, I'll be documenting the complete process of removing Google services from my workflow. YouTube is the last one standing.
- **Supporting the transition.** For those who are still on platforms that no longer serve them, I'll be sharing practical, step-by-step guides for building independent digital lives. The same brain-friendly, neurodivergent-centered approach, now applied to surviving a prairie burn.
- **Creating from the ashes.** The Surviving Capitalism Deck. New coaching frameworks. Resources built on infrastructure that doesn't feed surveillance systems or train AI on your thoughts without consent.

## A note about privilege, again

I said this in my [TikTok boycott post](/blog/off-tiktok-for-good-tech-ethics-and-boundaries) and I'll say it again: being able to leave platforms is a privilege. Not everyone can walk away from Reddit, from Google, from Meta. Some people depend on these platforms for work, for community, for survival. I'm not here to judge anyone who stays.

But I am here to say: the fire is coming whether we're on these platforms or not. The Wooden Snake spent a year showing us the cracks. The Fire Horse is about to show us what happens when those cracks split open. And the people who did the patient work of shedding, of building alternatives, of observing before acting, they'll be the ones who help rebuild from the fertile soil the fire leaves behind.

## The revolution isn't loud

I ended my first Wooden Snake post with this line: "The revolution isn't loud, it's unfolding in the gaps, cracks, and quiet spaces where creativity thrives."

I still believe that. The Fire Horse is loud. The combustion will be visible and dramatic and unavoidable. But the real revolution, the one that matters, that's still happening in the quiet. In the people building decentralized tools. In the communities moving to Bluesky and Signal and Mastodon. In the neurodivergent creators designing brain-friendly systems that don't depend on extractive platforms. In the daily practice of choosing where you show up and what you're willing to support.

The Wooden Snake taught us to shed with patience. The Fire Horse will teach us that sometimes, what you shed is exactly the fuel the world needed to burn away what no longer works.

Here's to the fire. Here's to what grows after.

Keep your code clean, your boundaries clear, and your firebreaks strong.
