# When Silicon Sheds: A Year of Wooden Snake Wisdom, the Final Shed, and What the Fire Horse Brings

Hey there, cosmic coders and fellow shedders.

I've deleted my Reddit accounts. All of them. And I'm not coming back.

This is the final skin shed of the Wooden Snake year—the last platform in a year-long process of consciously divesting from Big Tech. Meta, TikTok, and now Reddit. Google is next on my list for 2026. What started in my [Lunar New Year post](/blog/lunar-new-year-2025) as a year of patience, adaptability, and quiet transformation has become something more: a deliberate shedding of platforms that no longer align with my values, backed by real-world evidence that the tech industry's structure has been building toward something combustible for over a decade.

## The Wooden Snake's Wisdom: What We Carried Through 2025

When I wrote about the Wooden Snake year in January 2025, I framed it around three principles that have guided every post since:

**Observe Before You Judge.** Patient observation before action. In my [Rest Mode post](/blog/rest-mode-creativity-and-the-wooden-snake-shed), I described how six months of daily bullet journaling gave me space to reflect on the past year of skin shed before rushing to the next thing. That observation time was essential. I scanned and shredded years of paper clutter, finally making sense of my 10-year chronic pain journey—a physical manifestation of observing what I'd been holding onto, then letting go of what no longer serves.

**Adapt to the Tool's True Nature.** Work with reality, not against it. In [Back to Basics](/blog/back-to-basics-ai-ready-workflows), I applied this to digital organization: observe your current folder structure, adapt gradually with the 3-folder rule, transform quietly. The Snake teaches that true power comes from patient preparation, not rushing into action. In [AI Myth-Busting](/blog/ai-mythbusting-critical-thinking-toolkit), we adapted to what AI actually is—observing before trusting, finding smarter paths forward instead of brute force.

**Find Smarter Paths Forward.** Creative solutions that serve your goals. In Rest Mode, I gave projects like the Surviving Capitalism Deck space to develop properly instead of rushing to finish. The Wooden Snake's message from the [Lunar New Year post](/blog/lunar-new-year-2025) holds: _True power isn't about brute force—it's about adapting, observing, and finding smarter paths forward._

## The Shedding: What Actually Happened

The Wooden Snake year wasn't abstract philosophy. It was concrete divestment.

**Meta.** I left all Meta platforms as part of a larger digital spring cleaning—a shedding of platforms that no longer served me or aligned with my values. The [lawsuit alleging social media giants buried their own research on teen mental health harms](https://www.cnn.com/2025/11/25/tech/social-media-youth-mental-health-lawsuit-meta-tiktok-snap-youtube) reinforced that decision. Internal documents show Meta researchers acknowledged: "IG (Instagram) is a drug ... we're basically pushers." [Court filings allege Meta halted internal research](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/23/meta-internal-research-social-media-harm-court-filing.html) that suggested social media causes harm. [Federal judges rejected Meta's motions to dismiss](https://www.reuters.com/legal/meta-must-face-us-state-lawsuits-over-teen-social-media-addiction-2024-10-15/) lawsuits by 30+ states alleging the company designed addictive features targeting teens.

**TikTok.** When the [TikTok sale to Oracle and Jared Kushner's investment group](https://www.inc.com/bethany-mclean/larry-ellison-and-oracle-won-2025-but-at-what-cost/91263583) went through—investors reportedly paying $14 billion for operations valued at $30-35 billion—that was the dealbreaker. I wrote about it in [Off TikTok For Good](/blog/off-tiktok-for-good-tech-ethics-and-boundaries). I couldn't stay on a platform owned by the same people I've been avoiding across tech, media, and entertainment.

**Reddit.** And now, the final shed. Reddit joins the list. The platform's role in the broader data ecosystem—alongside Facebook and Google—has become impossible to ignore. When government agencies receive user data from platforms that claim to protect privacy, the trust is broken. The Wooden Snake's patience principle meant I observed this pattern for months before acting. The observation is complete. The shed is done.

**Google.** Planned for 2026. The Fire Horse year will include the next phase of this divestment.

## Why This Matters: The Societal Shifts

This isn't just personal preference. The evidence has been building.

**Data and AI training.** Meta's use of user data to train AI models has drawn scrutiny. When platforms train AI on everything we post, our creative work becomes their training set. The [Privacy Pleasure](/blog/privacy-pleasure-digital-independence) resource I created speaks to this: "Every face scan, every prompt, every photo sent to the cloud is data that billionaires are monetizing."

**Platform accountability.** Regulators are increasingly examining addictive design. The EU has explored legal action against features like infinite scrolling—treating engagement-maximizing interfaces as potential harms. When regulators start treating infinite scroll as a design liability, the industry's foundation shifts.

**Consolidation and influence.** The same power structures continue to concentrate across tech, media, and entertainment. [Jared Kushner's Affinity Partners](https://fortune.com/2025/12/08/jared-kushner-paramount-warner-netflix-trump-ellison-saudi-antitrust) is backing Paramount's $108 billion hostile bid for Warner Bros. Discovery alongside Saudi, Abu Dhabi, and Qatar sovereign wealth funds. [Kushner's firm joined the $55 billion agreement](https://www.forbes.com/sites/monicahunter-hart/2025/09/16/how-jared-kushners-bold-bets-in-the-middle-east-made-him-a-billionaire/) to take Electronic Arts private, set to complete in 2027. The same people. Holding more cards. The pattern repeats.

The structural problems in Big Tech aren't new. They've been building for 10-15 years. The Wooden Snake year was about getting past the itchy phase of missing a platform to achieve something else: peace from deleting your data. From choosing where you show up.

## The Fire Horse: What Comes Next

The Lunar New Year brings the **Fire Horse**—dynamic energy, independence, and passionate innovation. And here's what I'm carrying from the Wooden Snake into it:

The dead skin we've shed doesn't disappear. It becomes kindling.

I'm not predicting doom. I'm observing a pattern. When structural problems build for a decade, they don't resolve quietly. The Fire Horse year may bring what I've come to think of as a controlled prairie burn: the latent consequences of Big Tech's architecture bursting into visibility. It will look sudden. It won't be. It's the culmination of years of design choices, data practices, and political alignments.

For those still embedded in Meta, X, Reddit, and the rest—there will be disruption. Suffering, even. But prairie burns clear the ground. The nitrogen-rich ash creates fertile soil. What grows after can be different. Healthier. The EU's exploration of infinite scrolling liability could be one catalyst. Legal action against addictive design could shift what "normal" means. The re-emergence could be a reinvigoration.

The Wooden Snake's wisdom doesn't vanish in the Fire Horse. It shifts. We observed. We adapted. We found smarter paths. Now we charge forward with that knowledge—embracing dynamic energy, leading with independence, innovating with passion. The patience wasn't wasted. It was preparation.
